Our Story

Blending tradition with artificial intelligence.

The Art & Science of Indian Flavors

Founded in 2024, Lumina Indian Bistro started with a simple question: What happens when you combine generations of Indian culinary heritage with the precision of modern Artificial Intelligence?

The answer is a consistently perfect dining experience, every single time. Our AI models have analyzed millions of spice profiles, cooking temperatures, and slow-cooking techniques to discover the absolute optimal parameters for each traditional Indian dish on our menu.

But we haven't lost the human touch. Our passionate human chefs work hand-in-hand with our smart systems to bring you authentic aromas, warm smiles, and a cozy atmosphere that celebrates the spirit of India.
